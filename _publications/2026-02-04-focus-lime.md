Focus-LIME is a coarse-to-fine framework for surgical feature-level interpretation of long-context large language models. It uses a proxy model to curate the perturbation neighborhood, allowing the target model to perform fine-grained attribution only within the optimized context.

The paper was accepted at [IJCAI-ECAI 2026](https://2026.ijcai.org/accepted-papers/) and is also available on [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04607).
